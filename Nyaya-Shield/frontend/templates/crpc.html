Criminal Procedure Code (CrPC)

What is the CrPC?

The Code of Criminal Procedure (CrPC) is the main legislation on procedure for administration of substantive criminal law in India. It provides the machinery for investigation, arrest, bail, and trial of criminal offenses.

Why It's Important

It ensures fair and transparent criminal proceedings, protects the rights of the accused, and provides a framework for law enforcement agencies to maintain public order and safety.
